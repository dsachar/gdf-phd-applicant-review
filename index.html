How to export data from EUSurvey

Export your data as an Excel file and include uploaded attachments. Here is how:

  • 1 Access your survey: Log in to EUSurvey and open the specific survey you are managing.
  • 2 Go to Results: Click on the Results tab in the survey's top menu.
  • 3 Initiate Export: Click the Export button.
  • 4 Select Format: Choose Excel (.xlsx) as your desired data export format.
  • 5 Include Attachments: Ensure you check the option to include Uploaded files. EUSurvey will package everything into a single .zip archive.
  • 6 Download & Extract: Download the archive and extract it. Then select the .xlsx file using the button above to begin your review.
